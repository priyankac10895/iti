WELCOME TO ACMT GROUP OF COLLEGES

ACMT GROUP OF COLLEGES is running under Mind Power Educational Society (established in 2002), registered with Government under Society registration act XXI of 1860 Govt. of India.

The motto of ACMT is “Excellence in Management & Technology Education, Training, Consultancy and Research for Success”. And We are introducing new courses Engineering and management field like Aeronautical Engineering, Polytechnic, (Junior Engineering or diploma Engineering), Hotel Management & Hospitality Management and PGDCA(PG Diploma in Computer Applications), in lowest. fee structure and installment basis, which help to middle and lower class family for admission in Higher Education with Management and Technology.
Indian Companies are going abroad. So, we all are witnessing globalization and in this environment, the MASTERS IN MANAGEMENT & TECHNOLOGY are the most sought after human resources in the country.

Our Mission

To contribute to the development and wealth of the country by converting young generation graduates into value based, socially conscious, trustworthy and proactive student managers, Well Trained Aeronautical Engineers, aerospace Engineer, Master mind Junior Engineer, well Educated and trained Hospitality & Hotel manager.

Our Vision

To provide the environment of international stature to enable the student managers develop their capabilities to face challenges in the emerging global scenario.

Training Placement

In the context of quality and excellence, Training and placement department is a value addition of ACMT. With a view to improve the "Employment Skills" of students, Training & Placement department has evolved a programme, tailored to cater to the individual needs and potential of each student for the full length of the course. Ultimately this department envisages preparing even first generation students of rural background to meet the requirement of the job market.

ACMT which was established under mind power educational society, which is already working in the field of placement consultancy so we have great experience in the field of placement.
